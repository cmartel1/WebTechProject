Plan I plan to create a website that shows behind-the-scenes images, videos, and blogs regarding my YouTube portfolio.
Objective Right now, my audience only sees the final versions of the videos I publish. The objective is to share insights regarding how my videos are made and what transpires behind-the-scenes.
Target Audience My target audience includes supporters that are interested in learning more about my process. This audience includes people of all demographics, but especially college and high school students living in the United States or India. This website can also showcase information to brands that may want to collaborate with me.
Content I plan to feature blogs that share journal entries or behind-the-scenes details about how videos were filmed, a catalogue of my favorite videos, a gallery of behind-the-scenes images, and a page with information about my favorite creators and inspirations.
